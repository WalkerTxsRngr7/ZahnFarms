Artisinal Tequila from Jalisco, Mexico

While in Jalisco, I was learning about all the many steps involved in the production of a true, Artisinal Tequila. This Tequila comes direct from the producer. In fact, this is a picture of the pit where the "pina" (core of the cactus) is cooked before they can begin the process of fermentation. How incredible it was to be able to observe this process first hand!
