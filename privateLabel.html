Why go Private Label?

A private label brand, also kknown as a "store brand", is simply a product manufactured by one company and sold under another company's brand. Private label brands were initially introduced as a lower-price alternative to a national brand. Even when manufacturing costs are higher than a national brand, generally due to the difference in the quantity produced, the savings in marketing costs allow retailers to simultaneously sell the product at a lower retail price and make a higher gross margin on sales.

With our private label products, we still want to guarantee a quality that will become associated with the name AgSelect. Therefore, our team is still committed to visiting the source of all of your private label products, to ensure that you are receiving only the best, high-end products that will ultimately carry your very own brand on them!
